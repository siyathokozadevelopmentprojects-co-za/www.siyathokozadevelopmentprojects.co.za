About Us

"Courteous treatment will make a customer a walking advertisement"

Siyathokoza Development Projects has been sailing in the construction sea for over a decade and have been leaving memorable ripples as it gradually adventured in to the depth of this sphere. It is our wholehearted goal to become a leading young black empowerment and community development organization. We aim to continuously create jobs to the young and unemployed South Africans throughout the nation.

Our mission is to stand rigidly regarding our affirmative action in empowering our communities by creating an environment that will encourage the transfer of skills amongst individuals through skills development programs, capacity trading and other business programs advocated by us. We also aim to maintain a good business relationship with our clientele and staff members by executing our projects within agreed timeframes and service levels.
